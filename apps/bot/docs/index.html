Introducing LondonTransit

LondonTransit brings London's essential transport network to your discord server. It provides live information on London's buses, underground, overground, DLR, Elizabeth line, trams and river services.


Never miss your train when enthralled by chat antics

One simple command gets you all the latest departures from your chosen underground station. And with support for more modes coming soon, even the outermost areas of London are easily navigable.


Easily navigate London right within Discord

With our Journey Planner integration, LondonTransit provides a simple way to get from A to B, with support for all modes of transport, all neatly formatted in an easy-to-read embed.


Not just for nerds

Aside from other transport focused Discord bots, LondonTransit is for everyone. Whether you are indeed a transport enthusiast, or just want to know when your next train is, LondonTransit has you covered.
